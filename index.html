TOMATOES ARE MY JAM!

Step 1: Pick 

    Bugs were a bitch, but picking was great
    vibes and a good way to pass time in a
    covid world. All the plump firm tomatoes
    were taken by noon, so probably try and
    go early.

Step 2: COOK 

    Combine 2 pounds of tomatoes (cored, cut),
    1/2 cup of dark brown sugar, 2 tablespoons
    of apple cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon of
    fresh ginger, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1/2
    teaspoon of cumin,1/4 teaspoon of smoked
    paprika and 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper
    flakes in a Dutch oven. Bring to a boil
    over medium-high heat,stirring occasionally
    with a rubber spatula or wooden spoon.Simmer
    the jam until thickened, about 2 hours.
    Reduce the heat to medium and gently boil,
    stirring occasionally,until thick and jammy,
    about 2 hours.You'll know it's done when
    you scrape a spoon across the bottom of the
    pot and no liquid fills in the path. Refrigerate
    the tomato jam. Divide the jam between 2
    (8-ounce)canning jars. Cool,then seal and
    refrigerate for up to 2 weeks.

Step 3: JAR 

    The key to feeling legit are jars. I got mine
    from Michaels,but Target and other stores
    definitly have. I cut the bottom of a cup to
    make a funnel that helped ease the jam into
    the jars.

Step 4: ENJOY! 

    Simply incredible. Toast, butter, jam combo
    is like nothing else. I mixed my jam with some
    apricot jam and made the perfect jam hybrid.
